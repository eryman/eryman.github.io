About

Choir Director, Piano Accompanist, and Theory/Composition Teacher relocating to Houston, TX and looking for a full time position teaching Middle School/High School Choir, Theory, and/or General Music. Key accomplishments include four nominations for “Best Music Direction” at Metropolitan High School Theatre Awards and leading a high school women’s’ choir to the semifinals at the Intercollegiate High School a Cappella competition.
